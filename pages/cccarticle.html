Is there a discount for paying by cash, check, money, order, cashier's check, or wire?

Yes. We provide a 4.0% cash discount off our list/retail price to our customers if they pay for their orders by check, money order, cashier's check, bank wire, cash or trade. The prices shown for each product on the website include the 4.0% cash discount unless you choose to pay by credit card or PayPal.
