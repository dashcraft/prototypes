1 oz (Bullion Silver Rounds)

1 oz Bullion Silver Rounds - APMEX offers a large selection of products and many provide high-quality metals at often low costs. This category contains our bullion 1 oz Silver rounds that feature popular designs at lower premiums than many collectible Silver rounds.

In this category, you will find .999 fine Silver rounds from reputable manufacturers such as Sunshine Minting, Republic Metals Corporation, Heraeus and of course APMEX. The products below offer lunar designs, unique manufacturer designs and even designs based off U.S. coinage of the early 20th century.
