SWEEPSTAKES IS OPEN TO ALL PERSONS WHO ARE LEGAL U.S. RESIDENTS AND ARE AGE 21 OR OLDER. THIS SWEEPSTAKES IS VOID WHERE PROHIBITED. 1. In the APMEX Rule Britannia: The Great London Getaway Sweepstakes (“the Sweepstakes”), APMEX will award one individual two round-trip coach class tickets to London, England, departing from the closest major U.S. airport to the winner’s physical address, five nights’ accommodations in one double-occupancy room at a four-star London hotel, chauffeured transportation to and from the airport, transportation to and from the world-renowned Royal Mint in Wales for a one-day VIP tour, including lunch, and $1,000.00 USD spending money (“the Prize”).

PRIZE DETAILS

Travel will be in coach class on a major airline carrier of APMEX’s choice, departing from a U.S. airport of the winner’s choosing, destined for London, England and returning to the same airport as the departure. Winner and guest are responsible for arrangement of passports, visas and insurance.

Accommodations and transfer transportation within Great Britain will be chosen and provided on behalf of APMEX. The winners are responsible for cost and arrangements of their own meals and other incidentals, but will receive $1,000.00 USD. Winner must provide at least one month’s notice prior to desired travel dates.

Air travel arrangements are subject to the following conditions:

  1. Travel must be booked by December 1, 2016 and must be completed between SEPTEMBER 1, 2016 AND JUNE 1, 2017. Blackout dates include but are not limited to December 18, 2016 through January 3, 2017.
  2. The winner and companion must travel together on the same flights.
  3. All travel must be completed within seven days from the date your travel commences.
  4. Travel is valid for the segments stated above only.
  5. Tickets are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  6. Travel is subject to schedule changes and may only be used on the designated airline carrier’s operated services.
  7. This award is excluded from frequent flyer mileage credit and does not include carriage where the airline has seat lease agreements with other airlines (travel not valid on code-share flights).
  8. Prizes may not be sold to, bartered with, purchased or utilized by a third party.
  9. Government and airport taxes, and any other airlines surcharges imposed on passengers, are the responsibility of the winner.
