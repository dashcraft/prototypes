THE OFFICIAL APMEX 2016 BRITANNIA SWEEPSTAKES RULES

SWEEPSTAKES IS OPEN TO ALL PERSONS WHO ARE LEGAL U.S. RESIDENTS AND ARE AGE 21 OR OLDER. THIS SWEEPSTAKES IS VOID WHERE PROHIBITED. SWEEPSTAKES STARTS OCTOBER 22, 2015 AND ENDS NOVEMBER 10, 2015 AT MIDNIGHT (ET).


PRIZE DETAILS

1. Four winners will receive a tube containing 25 2016 1 oz Silver Britannias, manufactured by the Royal Mint and signed by Jody Clark, the artist behind the fifth effigy of Queen Elizabeth II.

2. How to Enter:
No purchase is required to participate in the Contest.

Beginning October 22, 2015, when a person pre-orders any 2016 Gold or Silver Britannia coin through www.APMEX.com, or orders these products from APMEX by telephone, he or she will automatically be entered into the Contest. Purchases made on eBay do not qualify for entry into the Contest.

However, no purchase is required to enter, so in lieu of purchasing the qualifying 2016 Gold or Silver Britannias, you may fill out this online entry form, providing your name and a valid email address. You may also send us a 3”x 5” notecard on which you have printed your name, mailing address and a valid email address. This should be sent to the following address and must be received no later than November 10, 2015. One online entry or mail-in entry per household. Send your mail-in entry to:

APMEX

Oklahoma City, OK 73102

If it is found that a person who does not meet the eligibility criteria set forth in these rules has submitted an entry, that entry will be deemed ineligible and the person will not be eligible to receive the Prize.
